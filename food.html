Spaguetti Carbonara Recipe

(4 servings)

Time

20 minutes

to simmer

Ingredients

  • 400 g of spaghetti Garofalo
  • 200 g of bacon
  • 50 g of Parmigiano Cheese
  • 20 g of Pecorino Cheese
  • 3 yolks and 1 whole egg
  • black pepper

Preparation

  1. Cut the the bacon into small strips and brown them in a frying pan, over a very low heat, so that they are slightly crispy, but without drying out
  2. Beat the eggs with a fork in a large bowl (which we will use to serve the pasta), along with the grated cheeses and a generous amount of freshly ground pepper, until creamy.
  3. Cook the pasta in boiling salted water, drain it and add it to the bowl with the cream of eggs, cheese and pepper without stopping stirring.
  4. Once mixed, add the hot bacon and a few tablespoons of the pasta cooking water, which we will have reserved beforehand.
  5. The heat from the pasta and the bacon will be enough to give the cream the ideal consistency, without the egg completely curdling and therefore without drying out.
  6. At the time of serving on each plate, you can add more grated cheese and more pepper.
